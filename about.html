Hello My name is Billie, I am a Bloody Mary addict from Melbourne Australia.
I have always been fond of a good Bloody Mary - regardless of hangover status or time of day.

Bloody Mary Melbourne was born in the summer of 2015 - A side project to my day job as a web designer, an excuse for weekend brunches and boozy afternoon adventures, and an ode to the best chilled breakfast drink in town.
It has seen a few different iterations over the years, and eventually I would like to make it world wide, an index for All of The Bloody Maryscrowed sourced, meaning - My viewers can submit tips and ratings, because I cant be everywhere all at once..


I rate 5 basic areas to give a total score out of 5.Taste, Size, Venue, Price and Garnish.I believe these factors all contribute to whether I would make the effort to come back to a place or tell my friends about. The key is in the balance of the flavors in the drink. It needs the right proportions of spice, sweetness, sharpness and sourness - and heaven forbid. NOT WATERY. An Awesome but not over the top garnish never goes astray and Size DOES matter.

Follow my adventures all over Melbourne (and further a field) in pursuit of happiness in a glass. Have a place you think I should check out? contact me here.

Long live the Bloody Mary.
Cheers. B
